Probably because it's fun and you can learn at the same time!

What do video games teach you?

Games nurture optimism and create positive emotions.

Gaming teaches players how to deal with frustration and anxiety.

Gaming promotes social skills; more than 70 percent of gamers play, either competitively or co-operatively, with a friend.

Games are being used to treat certain medical and mental health conditions.
